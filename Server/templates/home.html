Introduction

With SafeShare you can create temporary chatrooms, which allow you to quickly share texts and files across devices.


Room Types

Choose between an open and a secured room.

An open room can be accessed by anyone who has the room code and its messages are NOT secured with encryption.


A secured room is secured by a password. All of its messages get sent and stored in an encrypted state, which means that noone can secretly access their contents.


Find out more about the encryption process

Temporary

The rooms are only temporary and will close itself after 20 minutes deleting all the data contained.
